Encapsulation: 
    Wrapping up of data & methods under a single unit.

Constructor: 
    *Special method invoked automatically.
    *Same name as class.
    *Don't have return type.
    *Only called once, at object creation.
    *Memory allocation happens when constructor is called.

    It has three types-->
        1. Parameterised
        2. Non-Parameterised
        3. Copy Constructor

    Constructor overloading--> 
        Same name of constructor with different parameters.
